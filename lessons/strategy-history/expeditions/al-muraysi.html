📜 Expedition of Al-Muraysi' — 5 AH / 626 CE

Campaign Against Banu Mustaliq • Hypocrisy and Internal Dissent Exposed

يَا أَيُّهَا الَّذِينَ آمَنُوا إِن جَاءَكُمْ فَاسِقٌ بِنَبَإٍ فَتَبَيَّنُوا

📖 Quran 49:6: "O you who have believed, if a wicked person comes to you with information, investigate it."

This verse was revealed after the incident of ifk (slander), teaching Muslims to verify information before acting.

📜 Historical Background

The Cause

The Banu Mustaliq were a tribe allied with the Quraysh, located near the Red Sea coast. They were preparing to attack Medina, and the Prophet (ﷺ) led a pre-emptive expedition.

The Battle

The Muslims defeated the Banu Mustaliq in a brief engagement. Their leader, al-Harith ibn Abi Dirar, fled, and many prisoners were taken, including Juwayriyah, who later married the Prophet (ﷺ) and became a believer.

⚔️ The Incident of Ifk (The Slander)

On the return journey, the Prophet's (ﷺ) wife Aisha was accidentally left behind and was escorted back by Safwan ibn al-Mu'attal. The hypocrites, led by Abd Allah ibn Ubayy, spread slanderous rumors about her.

The slander caused great distress in the Muslim community. After a month, Allah revealed Quran 24:11-20, confirming Aisha's innocence and condemning the slanderers.

The hypocrites were exposed, and the Muslims learned a powerful lesson about the importance of verifying information and not spreading rumors.

🎯 Key Strategic Insights

📌 Internal Threats Are More Dangerous Than External Ones

The Banu Mustaliq were defeated easily, but the internal threat from the hypocrites (Munafiqun) was far more damaging to the Muslim community.

📌 The Hypocrite's Strategy

Abd Allah ibn Ubayy's strategy was to sow discord and weaken the Muslims from within. This is a classic "fifth column" tactic used by enemies throughout history.

📌 Divine Revelation as Final Arbiter

Allah revealed verses to resolve the dispute, demonstrating that divine guidance is the ultimate source of truth.

🎮 Game Theory Analysis

The hypocrites played a deception game — they pretended to be believers while secretly working to undermine the community. When exposed, their reputation was destroyed, and their influence diminished.

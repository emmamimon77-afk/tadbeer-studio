🇵🇸 Gaza & Palestinian Resistance

A historical overview of occupation, resistance, and the ongoing struggle for justice

مَا لَكُمْ لَا تُقَاتِلُونَ فِي سَبِيلِ اللَّهِ وَالْمُسْتَضْعَفِينَ

📖 Quran 4:75: "And what is [the matter] with you that you do not fight in the cause of Allah and for the oppressed?"

This verse establishes the Islamic obligation to defend the oppressed — a principle that underpins the Palestinian struggle.

🎯 Key Concepts

🏙️ The Occupation

Since 1967, Israel has occupied the West Bank, Gaza, and East Jerusalem. The International Court of Justice (ICJ) has ruled this occupation "unlawful" and "a form of apartheid."

🛡️ The Siege of Gaza

Since 2005, Gaza has been under a strict land, sea, and air blockade, described by the UN as "collective punishment." The blockade restricts food, water, medicine, and reconstruction materials.

⚔️ Resistance Strategies

Palestinian resistance has taken multiple forms: nonviolent protest (boycotts, marches, civil disobedience), armed resistance (military operations, rockets, tunnels), and international legal advocacy (ICJ, ICC).

🎮 Game Theory Analysis: Asymmetric Warfare

The conflict is a classic asymmetric game. Israel possesses overwhelming military superiority (air power, tanks, advanced missiles). Palestinian resistance relies on:

  • Cost-exchange ratio: Cheap rockets ($800) vs. Iron Dome interceptors ($50,000+)
  • Tunnel networks: Bypass technological superiority
  • International law: Use courts to expose violations
  • Civilian resistance: Nonviolent protests (Great March of Return)

The weaker side seeks to impose costs that make the stronger side's victory unsustainable.

🕋 Deceptive Ceasefires: The Quranic Warning

وَإِذَا قِيلَ لَهُمْ لَا تُفْسِدُوا فِي الْأَرْضِ قَالُوا إِنَّمَا نَحْنُ مُصْلِحُونَ

📖 Quran 2:11-12: "And when it is said to them, 'Do not cause corruption on the earth,' they say, 'We are only reformers.' Unquestionably, it is they who are the corrupters, but they do not perceive."

📜 Applying This to Gaza: Israel repeatedly offers "ceasefires" while continuing settlements, assassinations, and collective punishment. Over 50,000 Palestinians have been killed. Over 70% of buildings are destroyed. Yet they claim to want "peace."

The Quran teaches us that when oppressors kindle the fire of war while talking about peace, their words are deception. Muslims are not obligated to accept deceptive peace offers.

🕌 Islamic Perspectives on Resistance

أُذِنَ لِلَّذِينَ يُقَاتَلُونَ بِأَنَّهُمْ ظُلِمُوا

Quran 22:39-40: "Permission (to fight) is given to those fought against, because they have been wronged."

This was the FIRST verse permitting fighting — revealed when Muslims were expelled from their homes. Armed resistance against occupation is legitimate under Islamic law.

وَلَا تَقْتُلُوا أَنفُسَكُمْ

Quran 4:29: "Do not kill yourselves."

Suicide attacks are prohibited in Islam. Legitimate resistance must preserve the lives of the resisters.

📜 Scholarly Consensus

The International Islamic Fiqh Academy (2006) affirmed: "Legitimate resistance to occupation through legally accepted means is a right." This is the unanimous position of mainstream Islamic scholarship.

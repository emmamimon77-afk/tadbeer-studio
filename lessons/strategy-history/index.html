🕌 Islamic Perspectives on Modern Conflicts

Islam provides a framework for understanding conflict and peace:

  • Justice ('Adl): "O you who have believed, be persistently standing firm for Allah, witnesses in justice." (Quran 4:135)
  • Prohibition of Aggression: "Do not transgress. Indeed, Allah does not like transgressors." (Quran 2:190)
  • Defense of the Oppressed: "And what is [the matter] with you that you do not fight in the cause of Allah and for the oppressed?" (Quran 4:75)
  • Peace as the Goal: "If they incline to peace, then incline to it." (Quran 8:61)

Modern conflicts must be analyzed through both strategic and ethical lenses. Game theory helps us understand the dynamics of conflict; Islamic ethics guides us toward just resolutions.

📖 Islamic Eschatology: The Ten Major Signs

A comprehensive, evidence-based study of the final signs before the Last Day, derived from the Qur'an and authentic Sunnah.

Introduction: The Science of the Unseen

Belief in the Last Day is a fundamental pillar of Islamic faith. The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) did not leave his ummah in darkness regarding the future. He detailed the minor and major signs that would precede the Hour, allowing believers to recognize the times they live in.

The Prophet (ﷺ) said: "The Hour will not be established until you see ten signs..." and he mentioned them.
— Sahih Muslim (2901)

The minor signs are numerous and many have already occurred (spread of injustice, nakedness, competition in tall buildings). The major signs are ten profound events that will occur in rapid succession, transforming the world completely.

The Ten Major Signs (In Chronological Order)

1

The Appearance of the Mahdi (The Guided One)

A righteous leader from the descendants of the Prophet (ﷺ) will appear. His name will match the Prophet's name (Muhammad bin Abdullah). He will fill the earth with justice after it was filled with oppression. He will rule for seven years, and during his time, wealth will flow abundantly.

Evidence: "Even if only one day remains for the world, Allah will send a man from my Ahl al-Bayt who will fill it with justice as it was filled with injustice." — Sunan Abi Dawud (4282)
2

The Appearance of the Dajjal (The False Messiah)

The greatest trial (fitna) in human history. A one-eyed man claiming to be god. He will have miraculous powers (bringing rain, resurrecting the dead by satanic manipulation). He will travel the entire earth except Mecca and Medina. All believers are commanded to seek refuge in Allah from his fitna.

Evidence: "There has never been a fitna on this earth greater than the fitna of the Dajjal." — Sahih Muslim (2946)
3

The Descent of 'Isa (Jesus), Son of Mary

Prophet 'Isa (alayhi salaam) will descend from the heavens to the white minaret in Damascus. He will rule as a just judge, break the cross, kill the pig, abolish the jizya, and most importantly, he will pursue the Dajjal and kill him at the gate of Lud (Lod, Israel).

Evidence: "The Son of Mary will descend as a just ruler. He will break the cross, kill the pig, and abolish the jizya." — Sahih Bukhari (2476)
4

The Emergence of Yajuj wa Majuj (Gog and Magog)

Two massive, destructive tribes that have been contained behind a wall built by Dhul-Qarnayn. In the end times, they will break through and swarm down from every hill, drinking all water and causing devastation. Prophet 'Isa will lead the believers to a mountain where they pray for their destruction. Allah will send insects to kill them.

Evidence: "Every day, Gog and Magog try to dig through the wall. When they are about to break through, their leader says: 'Tomorrow we will break through.' But Allah returns it." — Tafsir al-Qurtubi, Surah al-Kahf (18:94-98)
5

The Rising of the Sun from the West

The first major sign that makes repentance impossible. When the sun rises from the West, the door of repentance is closed forever. Whoever believes after that will not benefit from their belief.

Evidence: "The first of the signs is the rising of the sun from the West." — Sahih Muslim (2901)
6

The Emergence of the Beast (Dabbat al-Ard)

A miraculous creature that will emerge from the earth and speak to mankind. It will mark the believers and the disbelievers, identifying who is truthful and who is not.

7

The Smoke (Dukhan)

A smoke that will fill the earth, causing believers to feel something like a cold, and disbelievers to be severely affected.

8-10

The Landslides, Fire, and Rapid Succession

Three massive landslides: one in the East, one in the West, and one in the Arabian Peninsula. Then a fire from Hadramawt (Yemen) will drive people to their final gathering place in the land of Sham (Greater Syria).

Strategic Foresight: The Treaty of Hudaybiyyah

How does this relate to game theory? The science of eschatology is about recognizing long-term patterns and preparing for the inevitable. The Prophet Muhammad (ﷺ) demonstrated the highest form of strategic thinking (Tadbeer) during the Treaty of Hudaybiyyah.

The Setup: In 628 CE, the Prophet (ﷺ) led 1,400 Muslims to Mecca for Umrah. They were blocked by the Quraysh. After negotiations, a treaty was signed that appeared humiliating to the Muslims:

  • They must return to Medina without performing Umrah.
  • If a Meccan became Muslim and fled to Medina, they would be returned to Mecca.
  • If a Medinan left Islam and went to Mecca, they would not be returned.

The Game Theory Analysis: The companions were shocked. They saw it as a loss. But the Prophet (ﷺ) saw the long-term payoff matrix:

  • Immediate Payoff: Humiliation, no Umrah.
  • Long-term Payoff: The Quraysh implicitly recognized Medina as a sovereign state. Warfare ceased. People could now listen to Islam without the "noise" of war. In the two years following the treaty, more people converted than in the previous 20 years.
  • Outcome: Mecca was conquered peacefully two years later.

Lesson for Today: True strategic wisdom (Tadbeer) is not about winning every battle. It is about understanding the endgame. The major signs of the Last Day are Allah's way of showing us the long-term payoff matrix of history. The believers who recognize the signs are the ones who will be prepared.

Glossary of Key Arabic Terms

  • Tadbeer (تدبير): Strategic foresight, planning, and management. The art of thinking several moves ahead.
  • Dajjal (دجال): The False Messiah. The greatest fitna in human history.
  • Yajuj wa Majuj (يأجوج ومأجوج): Gog and Magog. Two destructive tribes contained behind a wall.
  • Fitna (فتنة): Trial, persecution, or discord that tests one's faith.
  • Shura (شورى): Consultation. The Prophet's practice of seeking counsel from his companions.

📖 Islamic Framework: The Prophet (ﷺ) used multiple strategies depending on the situation:

  • 🕊️ Patience & Forgiveness: For personal offenses (Quran 42:40).
  • 🛡️ Self-Defense is Obligatory: "Whoever is killed in defense of his wealth, family, himself, or his religion is a martyr." (Hadith)
  • ⚔️ Permission to Fight: "Permission (to fight) is given to those fought against, because they have been wronged." (Quran 22:39-40)
  • 📜 Treaties must be honored — unless broken: "If you fear betrayal from a people, then throw back their treaty to them fairly." (Quran 8:58)

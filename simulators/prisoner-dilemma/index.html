🔐 Prisoner's Dilemma: Game Theory & Islamic Ethics

5 rounds. Your choices against an adaptive opponent. Learn cooperation, trust, and Islamic values.

وَتَعَاوَنُوا عَلَى الْبِرِّ وَالتَّقْوَىٰ

📖 Islamic Foundation: "And cooperate in righteousness and piety." (Quran 5:2) — Islam encourages cooperation (ta'awun), justice ('adl), and brotherhood (ukhuwah). Cheating, deception, and collusion are strictly prohibited.

The Prophet (ﷺ) said: "The believer is not one who deceives." In game theory, the Prisoner's Dilemma shows how rational self-interest can lead to mutual loss — but Islamic ethics point toward cooperation for the greater good.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: Shawwal 1447 AH / April 2026 CE

1. Our Commitment

At Tadbeer Studio, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information collected through our educational platform.

2. Information Collection

We do not collect any personal information. Our contact form uses Formspree, which forwards messages to our email. No data is stored in any database on this site.

The Global Crisis Monitor fetches live news feeds from external sources. No user data is collected or stored during this process.

3. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. This site uses only essential cookies for basic functionality (if any). No analytics or advertising cookies are used.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Formspree: Processes contact form submissions. Messages are sent to our email. View Formspree's Privacy Policy
  • GNews API & RSS feeds: Used for the Global Crisis Monitor. No user data is shared with these services.

5. Data Security

Because we collect no personal data, there is no data to secure. Contact form messages are transmitted via HTTPS and stored only in our email inbox.

6. Your Rights

Since we collect no personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. For any questions, please use our Contact page.

7. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
